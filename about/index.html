about
sbd 1 oak

SB Development (SBD) is a premier real estate developer that has constructed over 20 assets in the NY metropolitan area with a particular expertise in ground-up residential construction. SBD’s success over the past decade is due to employing a unique method of design-build by applying our construction experience, hands-on approach and strict adherence to schedule within the initial design phase through closeout. SBD focuses on designing and coordinating a project that makes sense from a builder’s and end-user’s perspective.

SB Development has over half million square-feet under construction and in predevelopment, ranging from intricate townhomes to high-rise towers. SBD was founded by real estate developers Roni Benjamini and Joseph Stern whose backgrounds combine engineering, construction, development, acquisitions and finance. SBD brings a wealth of experience in developing mixed-use and mixed income projects throughout the boroughs and greater NY metropolitan area.
The company was formed in 2010 and grew to a full-service development company over the last decade. Having constructed over two million square feet of commercial, alternate-use and residential properties to luxury residential, SB understands both the complexities of new construction as well as the need to live by a responsive decision-making process. 1 Oak Contracting is the GC arm of SB and is the foundation of SB’s «hands-on» project management approach through the creation of a collaborative atmosphere with the development team by focusing all efforts on common goals. SB Development + 1 Oak will schedule, oversee and coordinate the project from inception through close-out. At the heart of SB operations is the project schedule. In large part, our success has come from an approach to finishing projects on time, on budget and with the highest level of attention to detail.

Joseph Stern is a principal and co-founder of the company he built over the last decade. Joseph’s extensive knowledge in all aspects of the development process include assembling land, design, construction and financing, has successfully led to the development of a million square feet in various markets throughout NYC. Before founding the firm, Joseph was a principal at Jay Street Advisors, where he focused on providing development opportunities to the marketplace through the creation, in various forms of transferable air rights. Through this unique approach, Joseph has assembled several hundred million dollars in real estate transactions by applying an opportunistic view to real estate law, zoning compliance and tax incentives. Prior to starting Jay Street Advisors, Mr. Stern was an investment advisor and real estate consultant.

Roni Benjamini
Partner & Co-Founder
rb@sbdevelopmentgroup.com

Mr. Benjamini is a principal at SB Development and CEO of 1 Oak Construction. Roni has twenty years of hands-on construction and project management experience which started with best-in-class general contracting firms. Having constructed over two million square feet of commercial, alternate-use, luxury and affordable residential properties throughout the boroughs and greater NY metropolitan area. Before founding SB Development and 1 Oak, Mr. Benjamini worked at Rockrose Construction as the lead project manager for a 500-unit ground-up residential tower on the Queens waterfront. He successfully delivered this $110 million high-rise project within the projected 18 month timeframe. Prior to working for Rockrose, Mr. Benjamini worked at Turner Construction on the Goldman Sachs Office Tower in Jersey City and for Bovis Lend Lease on the Ben Gurion International Airport in Israel. Mr. Benjamini earned an MBA from the NYU Stern School of Business in 2005 and holds both a Master & Bachelor Degree in Engineering from The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art with concentrations in Geotechnical & Structural Engineering.
